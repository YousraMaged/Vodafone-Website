How do I deposit money in my Vodafone Cash account?

  • Simply visit any of the outlets with the Vodafone Cash sign, and deposit your money (with a maximum amount of 6,000 EGP/day and 5,0000 EGP/month).

  • You will receive a confirmation SMS on your mobile confirming successful deposit.

  • The maximum amount of money you can keep in your wallet is 10,000 EGP.

  • Win free units when you send or receive money through VF Cash.

  • Use Vodafone cash now to send or receive money and get the same value of your transaction as free telecom units. For example, if you transfer 1000 EGP, you’ll get 1000 free telecom units. When the receiver cashes out the transferred amount, they will get the same reward.

What are the fees?

Transfer Fees

Transfer fees are 1LE to be deducted from the remaining balance of your Vodafone Cash wallet.

Withdrawal Fees

Withdrawal fees are 2% of the withdrawal amount with a minimum of 3 LE. (i.e.: Withdrawal amounts between 5 LE and 150 LE are subject to 3LE cash out fees. Amounts above 150 LE are subject to 2% cash out fees.)
